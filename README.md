# ESG-Profitability-Dashboard-Using-Power-BI
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze the relationship between Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) metrics and financial performance across multiple companies, industries, and regions. The dashboard enables decision-makers to identify sustainability trends, benchmark ESG performance, and assess environmental impact on profitability.

Tools & Technologies:
   •	Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Visualizations)
   •	Excel / CSV (Data Source)
   •	DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for custom KPIs and comparisons
   •	Data Cleaning & Relationships within Power BI model view

Project Structure 
1. Summary Dashboard 
Provides an executive overview of company-wide ESG performance and profitability.
   •	Key Metrics: Average ESG Score, Profit Margin, Total Revenue, Carbon Emissions
   •	Visuals:
       o	ESG vs Profit Scatter Plot
       o	Avg ESG & Avg Profit by Industry
       o	KPI Cards
       o	Industry-wise comparison charts
   •	Slicers: Industry, Region, Year, Company

2. Company Drilldown View (Page 2)
Enables detailed company-wise analysis and benchmarking.
   •	Matrix Table:
       o	ESG Score, Profit Margin, Revenue, Emissions, Water & Energy Use
       o	Industry averages for ESG and Profit Margin for comparison
   •	Line Chart: ESG score trend over time
   •	Conditional formatting for highlighting performance vs industry average
   •	Slicers: Company Name, Industry, Region, Year

3. Regional & Environmental View (Page 3)
Offers a geographic and environmental impact overview.
   •	Map: Total Carbon Emissions by Region
   •	Clustered Bar Chart: Water vs Energy Consumption per Region
   •	Line Chart: ESG Trends by Region over Years
   •	Slicers: Industry, Year

Key Outcomes:
   •	Built a dynamic ESG vs Profitability dashboard from raw data
   •	Used DAX to calculate industry benchmarks, KPI comparisons, and environmental aggregations
   •	Applied conditional formatting and slicer interactions for deeper insights
   •	Enabled stakeholder-ready storytelling with multi-level drilldowns (company, industry, region)
   •	Strengthened understanding of sustainable data storytelling in business contexts
